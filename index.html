Hi we are Restaurant Direct. We are four high school students who joined this program called Timeraiser Accelerator at our school. The program allowed us to focus on a problem that we were passionate in solving and create a service that can help tackle that issue. During the Covid-19 pandemic, we noticed that restaurants have resorted to using third party applications such as Uber in order for people to order takeout online or for delivery. These third party applications essentially take a large commission out of the restaurants bill which can detrimentally impact a restaurant. Anyway, our team has worked on an online ordering service and platform that can help restaurants digitally connect with their customers and also let them keep their money. For people that use our app, they are able to get cheaper prices and be a part of our digital loyalty program.

Restaurant Direct's Mission

Our mission is to provide the best online ordering system for restaurants and the best online ordering experience for their customers. Our app is part of our initiative to help out restaurants during this pandemic and allow restaurants to take control of their own market again.
