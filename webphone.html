Our Mission

By removing hidden fees and allowing restaurants to keep all their profit a feeling of transparency is created. We thrive to cultivate this feeling and to allow restaurants to create an internet persona to connect with users and customers.

Our Story

During the pandemic, local restaurants struggled a lot. This caused third party ordering apps such as Ubereats or Doordash to be put under scrutiny. As a group of 14 year olds, we believed that it was wrong to charge 30 percent comission fees. Therefore, in 2020 we decided to start a personal project to create a food pickup app where local restaurants could thrive. Eventually the idea of restaurants being able to make their own brand and directly market to an app full of hungry users popped up. Eventually, after development and a lot of fixing, we were able to launch our app on both the Apple and Google play store.
